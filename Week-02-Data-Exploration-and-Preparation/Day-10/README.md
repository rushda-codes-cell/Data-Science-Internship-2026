# Day 10 – Inventory Optimization

## Objective

The objective of Day 10 was to optimize inventory levels using forecasted demand to improve stock management and reduce inventory-related costs.

---

## Tasks Performed

### 1. Demand Analysis

Analyzed forecasted product demand.

The following tasks were completed:

- Used demand forecasting results.
- Estimated future product demand.
- Identified high-demand products.

### 2. Inventory Planning

Implemented inventory optimization techniques.

The following tasks were completed:

- Calculated reorder points.
- Estimated safety stock.
- Suggested optimal inventory levels.

---

## Tools & Libraries Used

- Python
- Jupyter Notebook
- Pandas
- NumPy

---

## Outcome

By the end of Day 10, an inventory optimization strategy was developed to improve stock availability while minimizing inventory costs.
