# Day 4 – Time Series Data Preparation

## Objective

The objective of Day 4 was to prepare the retail sales dataset for time series forecasting. This involved organizing the data in chronological order, testing for stationarity, and decomposing the time series into its key components to better understand underlying patterns and trends.

---

## Tasks Performed

### 1. Time Series Data Preparation

Prepared the dataset for forecasting by converting transaction records into a structured time series format.

The following tasks were completed:

- Converted date columns into datetime format.
- Sorted the dataset by date.
- Aggregated sales data at regular time intervals.
- Set the date column as the index.
- Prepared the dataset for time series analysis.

### 2. Stationarity Testing

Performed stationarity tests to determine whether the time series data was suitable for forecasting models.

The following steps were carried out:

- Checked for trends and seasonality.
- Applied statistical tests to evaluate stationarity.
- Identified whether data transformation was required.

### 3. Time Series Decomposition

Decomposed the time series into different components to better understand sales behavior.

The decomposition included:

- Trend Component
- Seasonal Component
- Residual Component

## Outcome

By the end of Day 4, the retail sales data was successfully transformed into a time series format and analyzed for trends, seasonality, and stationarity. This prepared the dataset for building forecasting models in the next stage of the internship.
